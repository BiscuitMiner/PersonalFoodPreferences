Weak Satisfaction

Means the pawn consumed a preferred ingredient, but not a complete preferred meal.

For example, eating raw meat, raw fish, raw vegetables, or grains can temporarily satisfy a pawn's cravings and remove preference deprivation, but these foods do not provide the full emotional satisfaction of a proper meal and will not contribute to picky-eating buildup.

Recovery Restriction

Eating non-preferred raw ingredients or raw fruit does not count toward picky-eating recovery. The recovery counter only increments when a non-preferred proper meal (Meal) is consumed.

For example: a meat-preferring pawn with mild picky eating will not progress recovery by eating raw vegetables — they must eat a cooked meal made from vegetables.

Picky Eating Mechanism

Requires Dietary Variety to be enabled in mod settings.

Trigger

  • Mild: Eat preferred proper meals consecutively (default 5). Only Meal-level primary/fallback matches count.
  • Severe: Eat preferred proper meals consecutively (default 12).
  • Permanent: Eat preferred proper meals consecutively (default 20).

Only Meal-level preference matches (IsPrimaryMatch or IsFallbackMatch) accumulate picky-eating counters. Raw ingredients or Ingredient-level matches do not increase the tendency.

Recovery / Demotion

  • Mild → None: Eat non-preferred proper meals (default 3). Raw ingredients do not count.
  • Severe → Mild: Eat non-preferred proper meals (default 5). Raw ingredients do not count.
  • Permanent: Irreversible.

Recovery counters reset to zero if a preferred meal is eaten midway. Thresholds are configurable in mod settings.

Effects

  • Mild: Moving/Manipulation -5%, BloodFiltration/Immunity -10%
  • Severe: Moving/Manipulation -10%, BloodFiltration/Immunity -20%
  • Permanent: Moving/Manipulation -15%, BloodFiltration/Immunity -30%
  • Extra mood penalty when eating non-preferred food (configurable)

Preference Deprivation Mechanism

Requires Dietary Variety to be enabled in mod settings.

Trigger

  • Taste Fatigue: No preferred food for X days (default 15). Stage 0: -3 mood.
  • Dietary Aversion: No preferred food for X days (default 30). Stage 1: -8 mood.
  • Deprivation Incident: Beyond dietary aversion days, a random incident can fire, adding the Preference Deprivation hediff (Eating -30%) with possible mental break (hide in room / sad wander).

Removal

  • Eating any food that satisfies the preference (raw or cooked) immediately removes the hediff.
  • Proper meal: +20 mood (PFP_PreferenceSatisfied). Raw ingredient: +10 mood.
  • Day thresholds are configurable in mod settings.
